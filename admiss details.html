Admission Process (KCET & Management)

Step 1: KCET Counseling

Students must attend KEA counseling and choose the college during seat allotment.

Step 2: Document Verification

Submit required documents at the college admission office.

Step 3: Fee Payment

Pay the prescribed annual fees to confirm the admission.

Fee Structure

Documents Required for Admission

  • KCET Admit Card & Rank Card
  • 10th & 12th Marks Cards
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photos (4)
  • Aadhaar Card
